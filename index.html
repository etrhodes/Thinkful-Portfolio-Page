About Me

I am an outdoor enthusiast that loves to spend time hiking, fishing, and kayaking. I also enjoy traveling all over the world and exploring new places and cultures. Some places I have visited are:

  • Costa Rica
  • Thailand
  • Philippines
  • Spain
  • Portugal

Besides being a developer, I am a lifelong entrepreneur, having started my first business at the age of 17. Since then, I have started multiple businesses in various industries. My main industry being in the pet sitting business. I have locations in Santa Fe, NM, Colorado Springs, CO, and Kansas City, MO. Although I love working with animals, I am now working on a career change to spend more time with my family, and I couldn't be happier!

My journey with coding started with creating simple websites for other people. I mostly used website builders but had always been curious as to how to write code on my own. I love working and collaborating with others on projects to make sure what we are working on comes out the best way it possibly can. Hearing other's thoughts and ideas always pushes me to create something that I might not have thought of on my own.

As an engineer, I am excited about:

  • Creating websites for my clients
  • Writing code that is easy to understand
  • Working with like-minded people
  • Creating something others enjoy
